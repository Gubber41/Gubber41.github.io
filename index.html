OVERVIEW
A casual multiplayer 2D pixelated dungeon crawler RPG mobile party game with the aim to outlast your opponent/s, or make it to the third floor of the dungeon and beat the final boss before anyone else.
Throughout the dungeon you will find items and artifacts for your character to help you adventure through the caverns and hinder your enemies as they attempt to beat the dungeon before you.

With party game aspects, you will be able to see other players' screens alongside your own, allowing you to strategically play around your opponents, and strike them with items and abilities at the most opportune times.
The standard game will be formatted as a 1v1 with each player playing on their own device, but different game modes will allow players to explore unique and fun ways to play the game, such as a battle royale with many players. The aim of this game is to supply a group of friends with a fun party game that won't leave anyone out, and allow everyone in the group to play.
With many game modes allowing as many players as you want, nobody will be left out when a group of friends want to play. Whether you have just 2 or even 20 people, there will always be a game mode available to allow everyone to play at once whilst on the go.
CHARACTERS
 There are 4 playable characters in the game, including the Ranger, Necrobrawler, Mage, and Rogue.

Each character has a skill and an ultimate that give each character their own unique playstyle. The skill has a set cooldown, whereas the ultimate requires players to defeat enemies to charge up.

Hover over each character to see their abilities!

Ranger

Skill
Focus Fire: Enchant your bow to deal bonus damage for a short time.
Ultimate
Arrow Rain: Fire a barrage of arrows in a specified area, dealing large amounts of damage to enemies caught within.

Necrobrawler

Skill
Skull Smash: Smash the ground with your club, dealing damage and slowing all enemies near you.
Ultimate
Skeleton Wrath: Summon a skeleton in all opponents' dungeons with stats proportional to the amount of enemies defeated since your last ultimate.

Mage

Skill
Elemental Shield: Create a bubble around your character that absorbs damage for a short period.
Ultimate
Stormy Weather: Manifest a thundercloud above your head that follows you. Enemies that draw near are stunned and damaged by lightning.

Rogue

Skill
Sweeping Dash: Dash a short distance, dealing damage to any enemies in your path. For each enemy hit, gain a temporary speed increase.
Ultimate
The Perfect Crime: Steal a random artifact from an enemy and use it immediately. Then, reset all cooldowns.
GAMEPLAY
 Each player will be able to access their opponents' screens as well as their own.

The dungeon will be a series of rooms connected with maze-like hallways, and each room will close off behind you making you face whatever lies inside, good or bad. You may find powerful artifacts and items, or you may be ambushed by a horde of powerful enemies to fight off.

Artifacts are powerful and reusable tools that could deal damage to opponents or enemies, or give buffs to your own character.

Items are consumables such as health potions to be quickly used in dangerous situations.
